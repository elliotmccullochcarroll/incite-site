How it works

Uncertainty, made visible.

Every sentence Claude generates is scored against signals that predict where AI goes wrong most often.

01
🔍

Detects signals

Pattern matching and a background AI check scan each sentence for high-risk structures.

02
〰

Underlines risk

Flagged sentences are underlined directly in Claude's UI — no separate window, no copy-paste.

03
↗

Points to sources

Each signal links to the right place — PubMed for medical, Scholar for citations, Justia for legal.

04
✓

You decide

Resolve signals you've checked. The score updates live so you know what's left to verify.

Signal types

Trained on where AI fails.

Nine risk patterns — each targeting a category where AI models hallucinate or mislead most often.

Citations

Authors, journals, and studies that may be fabricated.

Medical

Drug names, dosages, and clinical claims.

Legal

Statutes, case law, and regulatory conclusions.

Entity stats

Specific figures attributed to named organisations.

Superlatives

"First", "only", "largest" — claims that require exhaustive knowledge.

Temporal

Time-sensitive claims that may have changed.

Hedging

The model signalling its own uncertainty.

Negated claims

Factual negations that are quietly easy to get wrong.

Math

Complexity formulas and approximations with hidden assumptions.

Get started in 60 seconds.

InCite is a developer preview. Install it manually in Chrome — no store required.

  1. 1 Download the zip below and unzip it anywhere on your machine.
  2. 2 Open chrome://extensions and turn on Developer mode (top right).
  3. 3 Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped extension folder.
  4. 4 Open claude.ai and start a conversation. Flagged sentences underline automatically.
