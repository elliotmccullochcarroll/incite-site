How it works

Uncertainty, made visible.

Every sentence Claude generates is scored in real time against a set of signals that predict where AI most commonly goes wrong.

Detects signals

Pattern matching and a background AI check scan each sentence for high-risk structures.

04

Signal types

Trained on where AI fails.

Nine distinct risk patterns — each one targeting a category where AI models hallucinate or mislead most often.

Citations

Authors, journals, and studies that may be fabricated.

Medical

Drug names, dosages, and clinical claims.

Legal

Statutes, case law, and regulatory conclusions.

Entity stats

Specific figures attributed to named organisations.

Superlatives

"First", "only", "largest" — claims that require exhaustive knowledge.

Temporal

Time-sensitive claims that may have changed.

Hedging

The model signalling its own uncertainty.

Negated claims

Factual negations that are easy to get quietly wrong.

Math

Complexity formulas and approximations with hidden assumptions.

Get started in 60 seconds.

InCite is a developer preview. Install it manually in Chrome — no store required.

  1. 1 Download the extension zip using the button below and unzip it.
  2. 2 Open chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode in the top right.
  3. 3 Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped extension folder.
  4. 4 Open claude.ai and start a conversation. Flagged sentences will underline automatically.
